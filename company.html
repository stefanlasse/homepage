In a nutshell

LK-Instruments is a start-up which was founded in 2014 by two physicists to fill up the lack of missing features of every-day used laboratory equipment. LK-Instruments believes not in making things better but in making better things.

The Company

Todays equipment is good. But not good enough for rocket science.

No sooner said than done. After spending several years in the Wrachtrup-Labs, University of Stuttgart, Germany, the idea was born to design a new generation of everyday-used lab equipment to fill up the lack of features which make life more pleasant in science.

By finishing the development of our very first produce, the Stepper Motor Controllers, LK Instruments was founded in 2014 to make these technologies available for scientists and developers all over the world.
