M101

... a pound of precision.

The LK-Instruments M101 is a robust and ball bearing mounted rotation stage designed for carrying optical elements. It can be mounted to the experimental setup in any orientation utilizing standard M6 screws, set screws, posts and post holders.

Features

 Hall-sensor controlled automatic zero-positioning
 Standard SM1-compatible mount for optical elements
 Angular resolution 0.3° (without microstepping)
 Extremely low backlash

Specifications

 1200 full steps per full rotation
 1.33 A motor current
 1.7 % step angle accuracy
 0.1° backlash
 0°C to 40°C temperature range

Applications

 Polarization adjustment
 Mirror positioning
 Grating positioning
 Neutral density filter positioning
