M101

... a pound of precision.

The LK-Instruments M101 is a robust and ball bearing mounted rotation stage designed for carrying optical elements. It can be mounted to the experiment from any axis with standard M6 screws, headless screws, posts and post holders.

Features

 Hall-sensor controlled automatic zero-positioning
 Standard SM1-compatible mount for optical elements
 Angular resolution ≤ 0.02°
 Extremely low backlash ≤ 0.1°

Specifications

 1200 full steps per full rotation
 up to 38400 steps full rotation with microstepping
 1.33 A motor current

Applications

 Polarization adjustment
