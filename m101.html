M101

... a pound of precision.

The LK-Instruments M101 stepper motor is a robust and ball bearing mounted rotation stage designed for carrying optical elements. It can be mounted to the experiment from any axis with standard M6 screws, headless screws, posts and post holders.

Features

 Extremely low backlash
 Standard SM1-compatible mount for optical elements
 Step resolution down to 0.02°
 Hall-sensor controlled automatic zero-positioning
